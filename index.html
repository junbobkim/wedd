Bride & Groom

To all those close to the couple would agree, the bride Amanda K. Haldorsen and the groom Junkyeong Kim share a whole lot in common, most importantly their devotion to one another as well as affinity for laughter. Their union is yet another reminder that people are not perfect nor are the many turns in the path of life. However, the loving Author of Life has a beautiful plan of redemption and restoration, which He freely grants to ALL who turn to Him. They are a story of His restoration.

Amanda K. Haldorsen

The oldest of three daughters to Arnold and Sharon Haldorsen, Amanda grew up spending equal amount of time in Texas and Louisiana. An avid traveler with keen interest in other cultures, Amanda's trek has led her coast to coast in the continental U.S., as well as all around the globe. A product of the Eighties, Amanda is often heard performing a musical score from the likes of Depeche Mode, Duran Duran, the Cure, and U2. Her love for music and art has been handed down to her daughter "Bunny," Samantha Kay as well as her brother Robbie. Almost inseparable to her husband, she juggles her responsibilities as an Editorial Assistant, a mother to human and furry kids alike, and a Melee Wizard Demon Huntress.

Junkyeong Kim

The younger son born to the father Gang-Bu Kim (1939 - 2015) and Kyeol-Ja Kang, Jun was a South Korea native but made his way to Texas as quickly as he could. When he's not burning the midnight oil on a regular basis, he dotes on his bride, spends time online with his brother Weon and childhood friend Sung-Hoon, and amuses himself on the teenage exubrance of his son Skyelar, as well as his furry kids Samson and Baije.
